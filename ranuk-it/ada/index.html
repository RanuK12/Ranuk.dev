04. FAQ

Does a compliance statement protect me legally?

It's not a legal shield, but it's the document your insurer, attorney, and potentially a court will ask for as evidence of good-faith compliance. Most drive-by complaints settle faster when the defendant can show an audit trail.

I already have an accessibility widget (accessiBe, UserWay). Am I covered?

No. Overlay widgets have been named in multiple ADA lawsuits and do not replace underlying WCAG conformance. The fixes I deliver touch the actual HTML/CSS/ARIA — the foundation courts and screen readers look at.

How long does remediation take?

Phase 1 ships in week 1. Phase 1+2 in 2 weeks. Full Compliance in 3 weeks. Timelines start the day the deposit clears.

What platforms do you support?

Static HTML, WordPress, Shopify, Webflow, Squarespace, custom React/Next. Remediation code is delivered in a format your developer (or ours) can apply directly.
